Few Words About This App

Our country has the most no. of people aged between 18-35. A large no. of voters appears to vote in world's largest democratic elections. But, a very large section of this young and eligible population could not vote. Reason being, lack of a system for distant voting.
Our app is an initiative in the direction of making a system for distant voting.
